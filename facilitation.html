Creating great meetings is both a science and an art.

We crave on-point, effective meetings.

We partner with you in designing a gathering that meets your immediate goals while simultaneously building relationships and skills that support you running your own meetings when we are not with you.

We start with the end in mind

We will focus on where you want to be when the meeting ends so if the meeting takes some needed twists or turns, we have our eyes on the horizon and can make sure you make conscious choices throughout whether to stay in-line with original objectives or accommodate changes in service of a bigger purpose or emergent priority.

We’ll drive so you can be all-in

We provide skilled meeting planning, agenda design, goals and outcomes planning and facilitation for short- and long-term dialogues such as creative brainstorming sessions, strategic planning retreats, and coalition gatherings. Our facilitation helps you focus on what you do best.
