Why Coaching

We have been shaped by our many experiences,
and we are shaping the experience of others

Coaching helps you intentionally hone your values-based leadership to have the impact you want.

Leading Is Challenging!

You know that scene in the Barbie movie where America Ferrera gives this moving monologue about how hard and contradictory it is to be a woman? I think of leadership and supervision similarly.

You have to inspire with big ideas but/and also make space for your team’s ideas. You have to delegate but/and not overwork your team. You have to give staff autonomy but/and accept responsibility for their failures. You have to be compassionate when people need slack but/and you have to then support the rest of the team picking up that slack. You have to be an awesome manager while still trying to get what you call “the actual work” done. It’s hard! And it can be a wonderful ride with support.

How Coaching Can Help

Coaching is a safe space to tell the truth about all of it. The kind of leader you aspire to be. What’s so hard that sometimes you are just plain out of ideas. And everything in between.

It is a place where you will be respected and supported.

Through assessment and reflective practices, we guide emerging and seasoned leaders in an exploration of their leadership style and their potential impact on others. Insights about motivations, work styles, unique strengths, and areas for potential growth, provide leaders opportunities to grow, to manage themselves and lead their programs/organizations more effectively. We run experiments – all to improve both the quality of your interactions with others and your own work experience.
