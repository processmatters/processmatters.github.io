Organization Development Looks Like…

Assessing Readiness

We encourage organizations to conduct a realistic assessment of their readiness for change, and then help them map out how to grow, shift, and direct organizational energy and resources, whether that means enhancing capacity for current efforts, or preparing to embark on a new direction.

Team Building

By applying models of group dynamics and group development, we help groups maximize their strengths and identify where they need to grow. Team building results in improved team effectiveness and a more rewarding group experience for all team members, which in turn enhances staff retention and commitment.

Strategic/Program Planning

We partner with individual leaders and organizations to strategize how to achieve intended results of new directions. We help groups clarify priorities, goals and objectives; imagine possibilities within their capacity; and develop effective, sustainable projects that take into account staff and organizational implications of change efforts.
